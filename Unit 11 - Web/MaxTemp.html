Max Temperature

The temperature decreases the further away north you are on December 5, 2019. On this particular day it is shown that there were no cities warmer than 10C north of the 60 latitude mark. Conversely, there were no cities colder than 0C south of the 20 latitude mark. This would indicate that the world was moving towards winter in the northern hemisphere and summer in the southern hemisphere. The warmest point in the world was around -30 latitude, where the coldest point in the world was around 65 latitude.

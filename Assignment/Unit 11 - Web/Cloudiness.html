Cloudiness

On December 5, 2019 the very north of the world was cloudy. Most cities north of 40 latitude were between 75% and 100% cloudy. This is different from south of the 0 latitude as those cities were mostly 0% cloudy. This indicates that if one was looking at the world from space that the upper half was considerably more cloudy that day than on the south side. This does not mean that there weren't clouds in the south, it just means that the graphical represenations show that there was no cloud in the majority of the cities south of 0 latitude.
